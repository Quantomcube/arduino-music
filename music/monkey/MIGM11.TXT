System Requirements:

- MIDI Playback Software capable of reading Type 1 Standard
    MIDI File format
- General MIDI sound device (Wave Table or better recommended)


This Standard MIDI File was recorded directly from LucasArts Entertainment's
"The Secret of Monkey Island" adventure game.  It has been converted from the
MT-32 version for playback on General MIDI sound cards.  A Wave Table or
better sound card is highly recommended for optimal playback.
